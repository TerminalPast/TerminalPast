Unity within        division

Many people belong to groups and communities on the internet, with their own ideas, views and mutually agreed upon interests.

On top of that there are sub-cultures within an already established community that it becomes hard to keep track of what you are really a part of.

It is difficult for people such as myself and others to find a group to call their own, attempting to be yourself while staying in the perceived good books of your peers and the public.

But...

Every now and then, Everyone around the world subconsciously agrees with one and other. For a brief and fleeting moment in time, be it a week, a month, years; everyone decided that a random image is what they can all relate to, what they can all find meaning in and resonate with on a personal level.

Not only does it bring people together, it also provides people with a universal platform to share their jokes, trolls, observations and feelings.

A template on which anyone can create something that people can relate to.

I find an indescribable beauty in that.

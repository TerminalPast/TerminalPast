What the nft's consists of

As a way to to celebrate the internet in it's vast and overwhelming glory, I have decided to create some digital artworks that capture that feeling of unity and relatability.

The art medium of ASCII was chosen to create these items. I find it fitting as the medium only exists due to the existence and prevalence of the internet within everyoneâ€™s daily lives.

Without the existence of technology and it's widespread adoption, ASCII art would not exist.

With each piece, only the letters used to commonly refer to the image are used within the art. Each nft is no more than 150 characters wide.

As well as that the font selected is a reproduction of the old bitmap DOS font to give it a more authentic feeling.

Each piece is made up of a background and foreground and given a fantasy colour scheme theme name, much like how you would apply a theme to a text editor: Obsidian, Monaki, Classic etc.

I want each one to feel like it belongs inside a terminal window.

Most of the pieces will be in a Scalable Vector Graphics form as to preserve the quality, without having to scale it up 500%.

You also get the raw ASCII text as a bonus, use it wherever you want!

However...

There will be points during the collection that feature completely unique animated pieces.

To recap on what you get...

  • A SVG ASCII art piece that is animated
  • A copy of the SVG ASCII art piece in a non-animated form
  • NFT is coloured and given a fantasy theme name that makes it unique
  • Raw ASCII text version of your nft to copy and paste anywhere
  • The knowledge that you are funding my next project
